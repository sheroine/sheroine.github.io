What is it?

This game is an individual project done for class. We had to remake the classic game Asteroids and put a spin on it. I made mine with the theme of ink and water. Players play as a tiny water drop that shoots even tinier water drops at ink blobs to break them apart. Since the ink is foreign, the player has to get rid of them and purify the water, thus the name "Purifier".

Features

  • The waterdrop bullets the player shoots out decelerates then finally stop then disappear, instead of going forever until hitting an ink blob or going beyond the screen. This provides more of a challenge in hitting ink blobs and also looks like a real water drop running on the surface of water, when it stops it merges back with the main body of water.
  • When the small ink blobs get destroyed, they have a chance to drop bits, which is a purple collectible. When you collect 15 of these bits, you will fill up the progress bar that activates drill mode.
  • Drill mode makes the player zoom forward at a higher speed than normal. Players cannot stop during drill mode and will always move forward, but they can still steer. During this time players will try to ram into as many ink blobs as they can to destroy them instead of shooting bullets to break them. Destroyed ink blobs also give 3 times more points in this mode.
Notes:

I was rushing to finish this project and unfortunately did not have enough time to fix a few mistakes. The GUI display for lives and charge are out of place and look different on different screen sizes. There are also certain values I would like to tweak afterwards when I am playing my own game, since it is a little hard to activate drill mode at the moment. I also noticed a small typo. But other than these mistakes, I am proud of how my game turned out. I would definitely come back to it to fix these mistakes and add more to the game later.

Tools used: Unity | Krita | Visual Studio | C#

The Game

It is a top-down puzzle game where players have to place down traffic lights and stop signs at road conjunctions to prevent car crashes. Players also have to do this on a budget as traffic lights and stop signs cost money to be placed. This is a game we came up with for a weekend long game jam with the theme "Everyday heroes".

My Role

I was only responsible for making all the assets only since at the time of the game jam I had no knowledge of how to use Unity and did not have the knowledge to code a working game.

Assets I made include:

  • Menu screen
  • Victory and Failure screen
  • Traffic light and stop signs
  • Road tiles
  • Building tiles surrounding roads
  • Car variations
  • Smoke cloud (appears when cars have crashed together)

Tools used: Unity | Krita | Visual Studio | C#

The Level

I made this level to try and challenge myself to keep things simple, try making a background for levels, and to finish it in a week. I also made this level to say hello to the freshmen coming to Art House in fall semester 2018, thus the name "Hello".

I challenged myself to keep it simple because I am prone to overscoping projects, so I needed to practice not overscoping and put in less deatails; I challenged myself to try making a background as I realised I have a bit of trouble making them, so again I made myself practice; I challenged myself to finish it in a week for the same reason of forcing myself to keep it simple and put in less deatails.

This end result I'd say, the background is not impressive or anything but it's a start, and unfortunately I didn't finish the level in a week, it took me instead 10 days to finish it, though overall it's not a bare and underdecorated level, and relies more on color than decorations.

Decorations

I kept to using the shapes the level editor provided as they are instead of combining them to form more complex decorations. The amount of decorations is also a minimum.

The Level

This level basically makes the player go through candy land while in a dream, therefore it is named "Sweet Dreams". The player changes their gamemode everytime they enter a new area which has a different theme of sweets or desserts. I did not make a background for this level and relied on the given backgrounds of the level editor, however this level is quite decoration heavy as I took around 2 months to slowly fill up the level with colorful desserts.

Decorations

The level is divided into 7 sections of different types of sweets or desserts. Some areas have a bit of a custom background but otherwise relies on a given background in the editor.

Areas include:

  • Candy and Caramel Waterfalls
  • Soda Jellyfish Lake
  • Chocolate and Cupcakes Land
  • Rock Crystal Candy Cave
  • Ice-cream and Cake
  • Waffles
  • Macaron Piles

This level used the soundtrack Dreams by Namice
